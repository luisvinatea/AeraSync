AeraSync Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 30, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to AeraSync, a tool for comparing aerators in shrimp farming through a step-by-step survey. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our application. We are committed to handling your information responsibly.

Data We Collect

When you use AeraSync and agree to data collection (via the optional disclosure dialog), we may collect anonymous, non-personally identifiable data, including:

  • Survey Inputs: Values you enter, such as farm area, water temperature, salinity, pond depth, shrimp biomass, aerator specifications (e.g., horsepower, price), and financial parameters (e.g., electricity cost, discount rate).
  • Calculated Results: Metrics generated from your inputs, such as the number of aerators needed or cost comparisons.

We do not collect personal information like your name, email, IP address, or location. All data is anonymized and cannot be linked to you.

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data solely for research to improve AeraSync. Anonymized and aggregated data helps us:

  • Understand usage patterns and common survey inputs.
  • Enhance the accuracy and functionality of the tool.
  • Optimize aerator comparison models for shrimp farming.

We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to third parties for any purpose.

Data Storage and Security

Data is stored securely using industry-standard measures to protect against unauthorized access or loss. As the data is anonymous, it is not linked to any individual. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

Data collection is optional. You can use AeraSync without agreeing to data collection by declining the disclosure dialog (if shown). Your results will still be displayed locally. If you agree, you can withdraw consent by resetting the app’s preferences, though previously collected anonymous data may remain in aggregated datasets.

Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable data protection laws. As our data is anonymous, most privacy rights (e.g., data access, deletion) may not apply, but we strive to maintain transparency and security.

Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: privacy@aerasync.app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here, with the “Last Updated” date revised. Please review periodically.
