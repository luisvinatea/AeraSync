AeraSync Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2025

Introduction

AeraSync is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our aerator comparison application for shrimp farming.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our application:

  • Survey Data: Information you provide about your farm specifications, aerator details, and financial parameters.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous information about how you interact with the application, including features used and time spent.
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device and browser.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our aerator comparison service
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize the application
  • To develop new features based on user needs
  • To troubleshoot technical issues

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data for research purposes to improve aquaculture practices.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we have about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Object to our use of your data

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@aerasync.com.
